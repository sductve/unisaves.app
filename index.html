Our story

Why we built
UniSaves

"As a student, I kept noticing that so many of my friends had no idea what their student ID card was actually worth. Cafes, gyms, bookshops, loads of places offer student discounts, but nobody really knows about them. The information was scattered, hard to find, and honestly pretty boring to look through. So I built UniSaves. A simple, beautiful map that shows you exactly where to go and what you'll save, right from your phone. Your student ID is worth more than you think. We just make it easy to use it."

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

What we collect

When you join our waitlist, we collect your email address only. When you use the app, we may collect your approximate location (to show nearby deals), your university email for verification, and anonymised usage data to improve the app.

How we use it

We use your email to send you launch updates and app notifications you've opted into. We use location data solely to show you relevant nearby deals and events — we never store or share your precise location history.

We never sell your data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. Ever. Business partners on the platform can list deals but have no access to your personal data.

Data storage

Your data is stored securely. We follow industry best practices to protect your information from unauthorised access.

Your rights

You can request to view, update, or delete your data at any time by emailing us at contact@unisaves.app. We'll respond within 5 business days.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at contact@unisaves.app
